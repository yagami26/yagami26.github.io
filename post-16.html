K-P opposition patterns in Chess

Feb 03, 2025, Posted in Chess

Opposition is a concept I always knew, but never really appreciated the beauty of it. Basically a king gains opposition, when it forces other king to move and not occupy an important square.

Take a look at this study for eg

Here is another study to appreciate the opposition concept.

As a general rule I think we should notice the following:

  • It's bad for enemy king to step in front of our promoting pawn
  • It's bad for our king be forced to lose opposition, because losing opposition forces us to move away.
