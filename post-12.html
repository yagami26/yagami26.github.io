A new beginning

Posted on Sat 10 August 2024 in Music

I could not write for a while, things were seriously fucked up, and I was very distracted. I couldn't focus on either the problem solving, nor at work, nor at any of my personal goals. The toxic relationships, the unresolved anger, everything needs an outlet, I think.

Tatiana caused a lot of it, Karin brought me peace and calm. In the end, I only realised I could not rely on either on them to solve my problems. I need to go back to my old self. I remember a time, when music was a need. It brought calm, an escapade from problems. Practicing guitar was fun. I still like playing guitar but these days I think my primary motivation is being a show off on social media. After all, we humans are attention seekers chuckles

I bought a new Fender CD-60 SCE last week, and it sounds really beautiful. I don't plan to upload anything on instagram for a while, I'll post it on my Youtube channel instead as none of the people I know follow me on Youtube. The page is created under an alias anyway.

I started learning the fingerstyle technique. I learnt songs like Gul by Anuv Jain, which actually use travis picking technique and it was a little tough too, but I feel that my right hand is getting the general hang of it. Right now I'm learning House of the Rising Sun (by Animals). It is a great introductory song to learn fingerstyle. I'll make a post and section on the blog for adding guitar songs.
