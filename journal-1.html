Self Loathing Friday

Posted on Fri 27 August 2021 in journal

It was a usual work day. Nothing good happened, nothing bad happened either. It's strange how I want to avoid the people I know now. When I was in depression, I was pretending all the time and hanging around people I don't even like. Maybe it was the fear of being left out alone. I think I am way past the worst stage of depression and have realized a few things about myself. It's getting better. I think I am slowly developing a backbone. I felt like writing this article when I checked my phone today. Entire week I didn't receive a call from any acquaintances or friends, family, relatives and only telemarketers. Felt heartbroken, a little bit.

But is it really that bad. NO. I have always wondered what being emotionally strong means. I can't say for sure if this is making me strong emotionally or am I just avoiding problems and running away from any sort of human interaction. I feel liberated. I don't carry any grudges. I don't carry the weight of any responsibilities. Developing a little bit of DGAF attitude as well. In a way, I am thankful to pandemic and forcing me to live alone and ponder over who I want to be.

Anyway, TGIF anime time now !
