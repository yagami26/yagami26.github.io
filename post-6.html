Mornings in an empty apartment

Posted on Tue 28 May 2024 in Random

I have been struggling with loneliness for several years now. I do miss having a partner in life, but I don't want to get married either. Probably because I feel if I get married in this emotional state, it will just be a patch-work/a band-aid and I'll never be able to fix the root of my emotional problem. I have always been a little distant to people, a little awkward. But I was really happy at some point in life being single, and not missing a companionship.

I do feel I have moved on from her, but I never moved on the feeling of being defeated by love. That someone I cared about so much from the bottom of my heart will never love me the same way I loved them. It has always been hard to accept it. And this feeling of needing someone to feel happy about myself has stayed since then. Yes, it's a little immature of me considering I'm already 30 years old. I really want to be the guy who is happy by themselves and once I have reached that emotional state it will be the right time for me to marry.

The steps I took to avoid feeling lonely are sort of like a patch-work too. While I can't write about that here, people close to me already know about it. And that trauma doesn't help either. I feel I'm slowly getting distant to my college friends too, because I don't find anything common to discuss with them anymore. It doesn't help when they remind of the fact that I'm growing distant. It's so hard to even cry, it's like a part of me has gone numb after her. I feel choked with emotions but do not find an outlet to resolve those pent up emotions.

Writing about it here, helps a little, as I express a little bit of anger here. And I always said, this blog is not for regular audience, it's only for my own personal consumption. I need to get started to work, the last thing I want is to have my work impacted too because of this. This morning is not so good, but it's still a day and I should get started.

Coffee helped a bit
